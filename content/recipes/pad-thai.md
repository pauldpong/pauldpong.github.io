## Ingredients

- Palm Sugar
- Tamarind
- Fish sauce
- Shallots
- Garlic
- Protein
- Pressed tofu
- Rice noodles
- Eggs
- Bean sprouts
- Chives
- Lime
- [OPTIONAL] Preserved sweet radish

# Steps

## Sauce

- Chop palm sugar, melt in pot and brown a bit
- Add water, fish sauce, and tamarind paste

## Assembly

- Soak noodles for 1 hour
- Stir fry protein, then shallots, garlic, tofu, radish
- Add noodles, and sauce
- Add eggs
- Near end of cooking, add bean sprouts and chives
