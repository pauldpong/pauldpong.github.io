## Ingredients

- 450g Cherry Tomatoes
- Oregano
- Shallots
- 1 head of garlic
- Fresh basil
- Heavy cream
- Olive oil
- Parmesan cheese

# Steps

- Oil a deep baking pan, put garlic, tomatoes, chopped shallots, and oregano
- Bake at 400F for 50 mins to 1h
- Add heavy cream, pasta water, fresh basil and immersion blend
